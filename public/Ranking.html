The Best Movies According to Adrian

My Top 3 Favorite Movies of All-Time.

Gone in 60 Seconds

I love the cars in this movie, and the stunts are explosive.

Avengers: The Infinity War

The first part of the epic conclusion to the Infinity Saga. All of the superheroes introduced through a number of movies over the past 10 years assemble to take on the Mad Titan, Thanos.

Spider-Man: No Way Home

Spider-Man faces his toughest battle yet. As he tampers with the multi-verse, he unleashes a cast of villians from different universes.
